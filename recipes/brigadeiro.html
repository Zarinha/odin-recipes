Brigadeiro's Recipe

Picture of Brigadeiro

Description

Brigadeiro is the best brazilian dessert

Traditional treats of kids birthday parties

Ingredients

  • 250 ml condensed milk
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 4 tbs cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients in a pan.
  2. Mix all ingredients
  3. When boiling put in low heat
  4. keep mixing until butter texture
  5. wait until cool down
  6. make balls using your hands
  7. use chocolate sprinkles to cover them
