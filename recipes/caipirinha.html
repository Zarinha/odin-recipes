Caipirinha's Recipe

Caipirinha's photo

Description

Caipirinha is the most famous brazilian drink

Lemon fresh cocktail

Ingredients

  • 1 jigger Cachaça
  • 3 tbs sugarcane
  • Ice cubes
  • 2 Lemons

Steps

  1. squeeze 1 lemon into a glass
  2. Mix the Cachaça with the lemon juice
  3. Add sugar and ice cubes
